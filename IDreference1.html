Mysterious Initials
"Someone had carved the letters JG into the stone just above the bedstead, leaving him to wonder what that was about"
- Mortal Instruments 4: City of Fallen Angels. Chapter 14, page 275.
This is when Jace is put in a cell in the Silent City.
Jessamine, crazy after Nate Gray at that time, wrote JG, Jessamine Gray, as her initials in the same cell in which Jace was being held.
